**Privacy Policy — TradLinker, TradAssist, TradCoord**

Effective date: February 26, 2026

**1. Controller & contact**  
Controller: TradOwner  
Privacy contact: [TradLinker Suite Support](https://discord.gg/c5zvbAWwu8)

**2. Data processed**  
Depending on the features used, the Bots may process:

Discord data (identifiers & metadata)
- technical identifiers (user, server, channel, message)
- configuration settings (languages, settings, groups, roles, etc.)
- information required for operation (for example quotas, abuse prevention, diagnostics)

Content
- message text to translate and/or relay
- possibly attachments/stickers when a feature requires them

Subscriptions / payment (if applicable)
- banking/payment data is processed by Stripe and is not stored by the Bots
- information strictly necessary to manage paid access may be processed, such as subscription status, technical subscription identifiers and, where applicable, the subscription email address transmitted by Stripe

**3. Purposes**
- provide the Bots’ features
- enforce usage limits and prevent abuse
- save and restore server configurations
- manage subscription-based access
- maintenance and security

**4. Legal basis**  
Processing is based on:
- performance of the requested service;
- legitimate interest (security, abuse prevention, stability, backups);
- and, when required, your consent.

**5. Recipients / processors**  
Data may pass through services necessary for operation, including:
- Discord;
- machine-translation services;
- Stripe (if subscribed);
- hosting / storage services.

**6. Transfers outside the EU**  
Some technical providers may process data outside the European Union. When this happens, appropriate safeguards are put in place in accordance with applicable law.

**7. Retention period**  
We only keep what is necessary:
- server configurations: while the Bot is used on the server, then for a reasonable period for continuity and backups;
- technical / anti-abuse data: according to operational need;
- subscription data: while the benefit is active and according to any legal obligations.

**8. Data visible in Discord**  
Depending on configuration, some features publish information inside the server (for example relayed messages, translated content, onboarding messages, poll results, logs). These items are visible to people who have access to the relevant channels.

**9. Security**  
Reasonable measures are implemented to protect data, without any guarantee of zero risk.

**10. Your rights**  
Depending on the applicable law, you may request access, rectification, deletion, objection, or restriction.  
To exercise your rights: contact TradOwner via https://discord.gg/c5zvbAWwu8 and indicate your Discord ID and/or the ID of the relevant server.  
You may also lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority, including the CNIL in France where applicable.
